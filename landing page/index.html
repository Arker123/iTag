Animal Welfare Society
IIT Ropar

Animal Welfare Society (AWS) is a student-faculty-run organization that aims to promote the ethical treatment of animals and raise awareness about animal welfare issues. AWS aims to build a supportive community of students interested in animal welfare, and aims to provid opportunities for members to network and connect with professionals in the field. Overall, the purpose of the society is to foster compassion and respect for animals and to work towards creating a more humane world for all living beings.
